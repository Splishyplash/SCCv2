Leo, The Fledgling Fighter

Species : Human

Likes : Eggs

Dislikes : Goats

Age : 17 (8/29)

Born in a town of only 30 residents, Leo has always yearned for excitement in life. At the age of 7 his village was attacked by bandits, but thanks to the kindness of a wandering knight they were unscathed. From that day on, Leo swore that he too would become someone capable of protecting the weak. With a set of adventure's gear found in a long forgotten wood, Leo sets off to train in the one place monsters are always found.
