Nia, The Bubbly Bastion

Species : Okeanid

Likes : Sweets

Dislikes : Sea Urchins

Age : 24 (5/09)

One of the strongest ocean dwellers you would ever meet, Nia is also one of the quietest. But don't let her soft exterior fool you. As daughter of the former captain of the royal guard, Nia, has been trained in fighting ever since she was a guppy. This made her a prime candidate for the first wave of Okeanid explores to venture topside towards the newly found isle of Goldspire. There she hopes to prove herself beyond her father's name and become the first Okeanid to successfully clear a Dungeon!
