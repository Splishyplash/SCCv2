Sally, The Salamander's Tongue

Species : Human

Likes : Reptiles

Dislikes : Authority

Age : 19 (03/12)

Inkchantment: A soaring dragon scribed on her tongue, known as "The Salamander's Mark"

Born under a fiery comet, Sallie was always destined for mayhem. A constant trouble maker at her orphanage, she was known for starting fights with anyone she considered a worthy opponent. After besting all that her hometown had to offer. Sallie set out to find new and exciting foes. When she encountered her first stain she barely escaped with her life. She soon began to realize how weak she truly was, and how desperate she was to find new strength. Her run in with the vagrant witch Gwen finally opened her eyes to what real strength looked like and gave her a path forward. Now as one of Gwen's three apprentices, Sallie continues to train towards her ultimate goal, to wreck havoc on the beast that bested her.
